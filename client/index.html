  • goal of the game is to collect all of the keys before the monster eats you. This is a 3 player online turnbased game where 2 players are the good guys and 1 player is the monster.
  • The left sidebar shows the progressbar and when it is moving up that is the time to move your character. Above the progressbar is your players face and above the face is your players stamina.
  • To use the powerups you must catch a diamond and then you will see a diamond icon on the top left corner and to use it you must press x and your stamina will increase in the next turn.
  • To restart all players must press r

When the time start players click on tiles to move there character

Button X enables your powerup and button R if all agree resets the game

The objective is to get all the keys before the monster eats you
